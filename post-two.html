7hubent Tech – Free Digital Advertising Platform

7hubent Editorial: posted on March 13, 2026

7hubent Tech (commonly known as 7hubent) is a Nigerian digital advertising and online marketing platform that provides free promotional opportunities for small businesses and entrepreneurs. The platform primarily distributes business advertisements through messaging applications and social media networks.

The platform operates mainly through WhatsApp-based advertising channels, where business advertisements are shared with a large audience via status channels, broadcast lists, and digital communities.

History

7hubent Tech was founded as an online initiative aimed at helping Nigerian entrepreneurs promote their businesses using social media–driven marketing channels. The platform emerged during the growth of social commerce in Nigeria, where messaging applications such as WhatsApp became widely used for business communication and product promotion.

Over time, the platform expanded its reach by building a network of WhatsApp audiences and publishing marketing resources through its website.

Services

Free Business Advertising

7hubent Tech allows entrepreneurs and small businesses to submit advertisements that are shared through its WhatsApp TV–style marketing channels. This service helps businesses gain online visibility without paying advertising fees.

WhatsApp TV Marketing

The platform operates a WhatsApp TV advertising model where advertisements are distributed through WhatsApp status updates, broadcast lists, and community groups. This method has become popular in Nigeria due to the widespread use of WhatsApp as a communication and business tool.

Digital Marketing Support

In addition to free advertising, 7hubent Tech also provides digital services such as:

• Content creation and blogging
• Digital marketing guidance
• Online brand promotion
• Technical writing services

Audience and Reach

According to information published on its official website, 7hubent Tech’s advertising network reaches more than 290,000 daily viewers, with a broader estimated audience approaching one million users across its combined social media channels.

Advertisements shared on the platform come from a variety of sectors, including:

• Fashion and lifestyle
• Technology products and services
• Food and catering businesses
• Small retail businesses
• Entertainment and social content

Role in Nigeria’s Digital Economy

Platforms such as 7hubent Tech reflect the growing influence of social commerce in Nigeria. Many entrepreneurs rely on messaging apps and social media platforms to promote products and services.

Because many small businesses cannot afford paid advertising on platforms operated by large companies such as Google or Meta, alternative promotion channels like 7hubent Tech have become valuable tools for local entrepreneurs.
