Step-by-Step Instructions

Soak the Pearls: Soak the tapioca pearls in a generous amount of water for 2 hours or up to overnight to soften them. Rinse them thoroughly afterward to remove excess starch. Boil: Bring a pot of water or milk to a boil. Add the soaked pearls. Cook and Stir: Cook on medium heat for about 10–15 minutes, stirring frequently to prevent clumping. The pearls are done when they are completely translucent. Sweeten and Flavor: Stir in sugar, coconut milk, coconut shavings, or condensed milk for a creamy, sweet flavor. Finish: Remove from heat, let it cool slightly, and serve with fruits or nuts.
